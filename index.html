About Us

JK E-Sevai Centre is a reliable service centre dedicated to assisting the public with government-related online services, documentation, and computer support.

We support individuals, students, workers, and families by helping them complete essential applications accurately and without confusion. Our services include PF claims, certificates, Aadhaar-related work, PAN and voter ID services, passport assistance, and other online government applications.

Along with digital services, we also provide Xerox, printing, scanning, and document handling services under one roof. Every application is handled carefully to reduce errors and delays.
